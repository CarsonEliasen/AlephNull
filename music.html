Aleph Null

The music of Carson Eliasen


Developed for architect, the goal of this album was to invoke desolate and alien worlds. Since architect lives in geometry that cannot exist in our world, it only felt right to make the user feel like a stranger through the music.


Passage was Carson's first real dive into making music. Inspired by ambient artists he loved, he was sure that the only way to make music he was proud of was to just start. Scrappy, drone-heavy, and using samples recorded from his third-floor apartment: This is the space where Passage was born.
