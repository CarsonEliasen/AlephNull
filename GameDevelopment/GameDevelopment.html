architect

architect is a relaxing game where you build interesting scenes out of impossible geometry inspired by Monument Valley, M.C. Escher, and Oskar Reutersvard. Build mind-bending structures and populate them with strange creatures.



harmony

harmony is a two player board game that described briefly as a mix between chess and connect four. Players must use their pieces as well as their opponent's to try and match an alternating pattern of 4.
